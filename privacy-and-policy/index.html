Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Khetrofol Teleprompter mobile application and associated web services.

At Khetrofol, we create and upload captivating mystery and educational explanation videos in Bengali. Our applications are built to assist content creators, speakers, and video producers in reading their scripts seamlessly.

We understand that privacy is crucial. This Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your personal rights. By downloading, installing, or using the Khetrofol Teleprompter mobile application, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Age Requirement & Rating

Viewer and User Discretion Advice (13+):

Our content focuses on mysteries, scientific explorations, historical mysteries, and investigative explanations. Due to the analytical and sometimes suspenseful nature of these topics, the Khetrofol Teleprompter app and Khetrofol video content are rated and designed for individuals who are at least 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

2. Information We Collect

The Khetrofol Teleprompter application is designed as a secure, local-first utility.

  • Local Storage (Scripts): Any scripts you write, paste, or import into the app are stored strictly on your local device. We do not upload or store your scripts on any external servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous diagnostic information (such as operating system version, app version, and crash logs) solely to diagnose technical issues and optimize performance.
  • Cookies & Trackers: We do not use third-party tracking cookies to collect personally identifiable information.

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited anonymous device information collected is processed to:

  • Maintain, update, and improve the performance of Khetrofol Teleprompter.
  • Enhance user interfaces, responsive designs, and orientation scaling.
  • Detect, prevent, and fix critical software errors and crashes.

Our application and official channels (such as our YouTube and Facebook profiles) may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. We advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every platform you visit. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites or services.

5. Information Security

The security of your data is of paramount importance to us. We implement standard security procedures on our local databases to safeguard your configuration presets and saved scripts. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.
